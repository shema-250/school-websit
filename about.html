About Orpcare Secondary School

Empowering students with knowledge, values, and vision since 2010.

Our History

Orpcare Secondary School was founded in 2010 with a mission to provide high-quality education to the youth of our community. Over the years, we have grown from a modest beginning to a well-established institution known for academic excellence, discipline, and innovation. We take pride in nurturing responsible future leaders.

What We Believe

Mission

To foster learning that empowers every student to thrive in academics and life through critical thinking and compassion.

Vision

To be a top-tier African school, recognized for producing responsible, innovative, and globally competent graduates.

Core Values

Excellence, Respect, Integrity, Innovation, and Service define who we are and how we lead.
